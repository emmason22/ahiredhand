What Is Included by Service Type

Use this quick guide to compare options and choose the cleaning plan that best fits your home right now.

Recurring Cleaning

  • Routine upkeep for kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, and living spaces.
  • Dusting, vacuuming, mopping, straightening, and wiping down main surfaces.
  • Best fit for homeowners who want a home kept consistently clean.

Deep Cleaning

  • More detailed attention for homes that need a stronger reset.
  • Useful before hosting, after a busy stretch, or when routine cleaning has been delayed.
  • Often a good starting point before moving into recurring service.

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

  • For apartments, condos, and houses being prepared before or after a move.
  • Helps make a home feel fresh, reset, and ready for the next stage.
  • Best when you want more than a quick surface cleanup.

Make Ready Cleaning

  • Focused on getting a home neat, fresh, and presentation-ready.
  • Useful for transitions, turnovers, or homes that need to look their best.
  • Can be discussed as part of the quote based on your goals for the property.

Our Standard Cleaning Checklist (50 Items)

This checklist shows exactly what is handled in a standard visit, so you know what to expect before booking. If you are in a transition period, we can also handle move-in and move-out cleaning for apartments, condos, and houses.

Kitchen

  • Appliance exteriors cleaned (interiors upon request)
  • Microwave exterior/interior cleaned
  • Tables and chairs cleaned
  • Countertops and backsplash disinfected (all items removed and replaced)
  • Cabinet fronts cleaned
  • Sinks scrubbed and disinfected (dishes upon request)
  • Floor vacuumed and/or washed
  • Windowsills cleaned (blinds dusted upon request)
  • Baseboards dusted
  • Fingerprints removed from all woodwork, doorframes and switch plates
  • Trash emptied

Bathrooms

  • Tub and shower tiles scrubbed, disinfected and rinsed
  • Toilets disinfected inside and out
  • Chrome fixtures cleaned and shined
  • Towels neatly hung and folded
  • Countertops disinfected (all items removed and replaced)
  • Cabinet front cleaned
  • Sinks scrubbed and disinfected
  • Floors vacuumed and/or washed
  • Windowsills cleaned (blinds dusted upon request)
  • Baseboards dusted
  • Trash emptied
  • Mirrors cleaned

Bedrooms

  • Beds made (linens changed upon request)
  • Lamps cleaned and lampshades dusted
  • Picture frames dusted
  • Furniture dusted - top, front and underneath
  • Floors vacuumed and/or washed (under bed if accessible)
  • Windowsills cleaned (blinds dusted upon request)
  • Baseboards dusted
  • Fingerprints removed from all woodwork, doorframes and switch plates
  • Trash emptied
  • Mirrors cleaned
  • General straightening completed

Other Living Areas, Including Hallways and Stairs

  • Upholstered furniture vacuumed
  • Cushions and pillows fluffed and straightened
  • Ceiling fans dusted (height restrictions apply)
  • Lamps cleaned and lampshades dusted
  • Picture frames dusted
  • Furniture dusted - on top, on front and underneath
  • Hardwood floors vacuumed and/or washed
  • Tile floors vacuumed and/or washed
  • Hallways and stairs vacuumed and/or washed
  • Carpets vacuumed
  • Windowsills cleaned (blinds dusted upon request)
  • Baseboards dusted
  • Fingerprints removed from all woodwork, doorframes and switch plates
  • Trash emptied
  • General straightening completed
